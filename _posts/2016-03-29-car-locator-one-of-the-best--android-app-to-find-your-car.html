Car Locator is an Android app that allows you to mark where you parked quick and easy via gps. It will later guide you back to that point.

Car Locator is available in the Android Market here and is currently priced at $3.99: https://play.google.com/store/apps/de...

There is also a trial version for FREE here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/de...

There is also an Evernote plug-in for $1.99 for those interested here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/de...

All apps are also currently available on the Amazon Appstore for the same price.
